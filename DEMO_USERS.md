## Additional Information

- **User IDs**:

  - Faculty: FAC001
  - Student: STU001
  - Admin: ADMIN001
  - General User: GEN001

- **Roles**:
  - Faculty email (@ewubd.edu) will automatically be assigned the "faculty" role
  - Student email (@std.ewubd.edu) will automatically be assigned the "student" role
  - Admin email (admin@ewubd.edu) will automatically be assigned the "admin" role
  - General user email (non-EWU domain) will be assigned the default "student" role
- **Access Levels**:
  - Faculty, Students, and Admin users will have access to create and draft content
  - General users (non-EWU domain) will see a general page without create and draft buttons

These credentials can be used to register accounts in the system. The role is automatically determined based on the email domain during registration.
